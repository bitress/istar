About Us

Background

The International Society of Teachers, Administrators and Researchers, Inc. was established in the Kingdom of Thailand on May 19, 2019 through the leadership of its founding president, Dr. Rommel V. Tabula, a Filipino educator and researcher.

ISTAR is legally operating through its DBD Registration No. 0203564000907 in the Kingdom of Thailand, SEC Registration No. CN202103568 in the Republic of the Philippines, and CPD Accreditation No. 778922 issued by CPD Group, UK.

Vision

To be a leading professional, ethical and multidisciplinary international organization for global collaboration.

Mission

  • Establish and maintain a Quality Management System that ensures the quality of services that meet clients' satisfaction.
  • Exchange best practices in instruction, research, publication, innovation, and administration.
  • Organize conferences that promote new knowledge and best practices.
  • Recognize meritorious achievements of institutional members in instruction, administration, research, and publication.
