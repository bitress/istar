Guidelines for Honorary Membership Application

Qualifications of Honorary Member Candidate:

  • Non-Thai and non-Filipino member applicants must have a valid passport.
  • Filipino member applicant from the Department of Education must be a licensed professional teacher and must have at least 5-year teaching experience.
  • Filipino member applicants from State Universities and Colleges (SUCs), Private Higher Education Institutions (PHEIs), and Local Colleges and Universities (LCUs) must have rendered 5 years of service in their own respective institutions and must possess a professional license in their own respective disciplines and/or master’s degree or higher.
  • All honorary member candidates must have been nominated by an officer and must have served the organization in the capacity of a speaker.
  • The honorary member candidate must have presented papers in an international conference.
  • The honorary member candidate must have conducted researches and must have published at least one research in an online reputable journal.

Benefits of an Honorary Member:

  • Speakership exposures and leadership opportunities.
  • Assistance in research and publication.
  • Networking with local and international scholars.
  • Collaboration with local and international learning institutions.
  • Unlimited attendance to international webicons.
  • Awards and recognitions for meritorious achievements.
  • Discounted rates in seminars and conferences.

Procedures:

  1. The nominator should submit the filled-out membership application form together with supporting documents through istarconferences@gmail.com
  2. The president and CEO will determine the type of honorary membership to be granted: Regular Honorary Membership (1 year) and Lifetime Honorary Membership (no expiration).
  3. Certificate of Membership and proof of the organization’s registration will be provided upon successful submission of the documents for membership application.

Guidelines for Individual Membership Application

Qualifications of Member Applicant:

  • Non-Thai and non-Filipino member applicants must have a valid passport.
  • Filipino member applicant from the Department of Education must be a licensed professional teacher and must have at least 1 year teaching experience.
  • Filipino member applicants from State Universities and Colleges (SUCs), Private Higher Education Institutions (PHEIs), and Local Colleges and Universities (LCUs) must possess a professional license in their own respective disciplines and/or master’s degree or higher.
  • All member applicants must have conducted at least 1 research and have published in an online reputable journal.

Benefits of a Member:

  • Speakership exposures and leadership opportunities.
  • Assistance in research and publication.
  • Networking with local and international scholars.
  • Collaboration with local and international learning institutions.
  • Unlimited attendance to international webicons.
  • Awards and recognitions for meritorious achievements.
  • Discounted rates in seminars and conferences.

Procedures:

  1. Member applicant should fill out the membership application form. Tick on the type of membership: Lifetime Membership fee is 2,000 Thai baht while Regular Membership fee is 700 Thai baht.
  2. The membership applicant should transfer the membership application fee to the bank account number or GCash number provided by the organization.
  3. The filled-out membership application form and the proof of payment must be emailed to istarconferences@gmail.com.
  4. Certificate of Membership, official receipt, and proof of the organization’s registration will be provided upon successful submission of the documents for membership application.
